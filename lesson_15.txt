You can already punctuate your sentences, but now it's time to add some expression. Exclamation point and the question mark are the secondary uses of their keys, meaning that you must hold the shift key while typing them. For ! hold the shift key with your right pinky while reaching up to the 1(!) key in the number row with your left pinky. To type ? hold shift with the left pinky while reaching down to the /(?) key with your right pinky.
